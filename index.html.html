Our Story

More Than a Meal,
It's a Delite

Nestled on NH44 in Medchal, Delite Kitchen has become the go-to destination for families, friends, and food lovers across Hyderabad. With a warm, welcoming ambience and a kitchen that never cuts corners, every visit feels like a homecoming.

From the rich smoky aroma of our Chicken Lababdar to perfectly flaky Butter Naan straight off the tandoor — our chefs bring decades of culinary heritage to every plate. Great food, honest prices, and service that makes you smile.
